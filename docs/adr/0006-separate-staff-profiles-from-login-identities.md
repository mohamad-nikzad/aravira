# Separate Staff Profiles from login identities

A salon owns its Staff Profiles, schedules, capabilities, and operational history, while each staff member owns their verified login identity. Assisted setup may create an unclaimed Staff Profile with a phone number but never a password or verified user; the staff member claims it through OTP on first login and sets a password only when no user identity exists. Until multi-salon staff is supported, one identity may claim only one salon's Staff Profile at a time; a verified Staff Access Transfer detaches login access from the previous profile without deleting that salon's records. We rejected admin-created credentials and admin-controlled identity transfers because both confuse salon-owned operational data with a person's authentication identity.
